Monthly Customer Statement

Access monthly customer report for easy analysis

Manage your monthly reports with no effort. Generate customer's monthly statements with opening and closing balances. To get started, install Invoicing App and Customer Statement Monthly Module.


Start your free trial

Go to general settings, under Users and Companies select Users. For a specific user enable Monthly Customer Statement. Now, you will be able to access this wonderful feature from the invoicing app.

General settings >> Users and Companies >> Users >> Select_your_user >> Edit >> Enable Monthly Customer Statement

Now, move to invoicing app, under reporting, you will find Monthly Customer Statement.

apps >> invoicing >> reports >> Monthly Customer Statement

You will find customer statements already generated till date, isn't that amazing? Well, this is due to the one time scheduler executed at the time of its installment.

Not to worry about new invoices and credit notes created after installing our module, we have a daily scheduler that can be activated from your general settings.

General settings >> Technical >> Scheduled Actions >> Customer Statement: run scheduler daily

You might think of creating a monthly statement for a specific customer for a particular month. That is possible too!

Go to Monthly Customer Statement and tap on create to get your customized monthly statement of a customer for the required month.

Apps >> invoicing >> reports >> Monthly Customer Statement >> Create

Well, if you are stuck, here is a small guide on filling the form.

Under Month field select month and year as required and for Customer field select your customer. Do not bother about other fields.

Once these two fields are set, click on Reload button. All the invoices and credit notes of that particular customer within the mentioned month will be displayed.

It's not over yet! There is one more thing you might want to know.

It also gives you a feature to download the monthly customer statement in pdf form. This will make it very easier for you to share monthly customer statements with your customers.

To download the report, tap on Print option and select Monthly Customer Statement.

Print >> Monthly Customer Statement

A pdf will be downloaded.
