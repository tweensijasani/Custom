Why Round Off Value?

Let me first give you an overview of this module.
This module will load all invoices and credits notes at one place and calculate the total credit and debit of a customer every month. It is very tedious work to find all invoices and credit notes of a specific customer of the required month from all the invoices of various customers and of various time periods. Not only that, once you find them, you have to manually calculate the total debit and credit for each customer for each month.

In order to lessen your work, we have bought this app that will fetch all invoices as well as credit notes of a specific customer and calculate the total debit and credit with the opening and closing balances for that month. This will save you a lot of time, which you can utilize to enhance your business.

There is one more perk of using this app. It allows you to download the monthly customer statement in pdf form, so that you can easily share it with your customers and save time explaining and analyzing monthly customer balances.

To begin with, have the Customer Statement Monthly downloaded in your system. For its proper setup, go through the below steps.

Monthly Customer Statement Module
To begin with, you need to install the Monthly Customer Statement App from Odoo Apps. It's easy to locate, just search on the name, you can identify it with the Odoolicious logo. Hold on, you also need to enable it.

Apps >> Monthly Customer Statement >> Install

Enable this feature
Go to general settings, under Users and Companies select Users. For a specific user enable Monthly Customer Statement. Now, you will be able to access this wonderful feature from the invoicing app.
General settings >> Users and Companies >> Users >> Select_your_user >> Edit >> Enable Monthly Customer Statement

Scheduled Action
The one time scheduler executed at the time of installment will automatically generate all monthly customer statements till date.
Not to worry about new invoices and credit notes created after installing our module, we have a daily scheduler that can be activated from your general settings.

General settings >> Technical >> Scheduled Actions >> Customer Statement: run scheduler daily Now you are ready to utilise this feature. For more information go through the Work Flow.

Create Form
You might think of manually creating a monthly statement for a specific customer for a particular month. That is possible too!
Go to Monthly Customer Statement and tap on create to get your customized monthly statement of a customer for the required month.

Apps >> invoicing >> reports >> Monthly Customer Statement >> Create

Reload button action
Well, if you are stuck, here is a small guide on filling the form.
Under Month field select month and year as required and for Customer field select your customer. Do not bother about other fields.

Once these two fields are set, click on Reload button. All the invoices and credit notes of that particular customer within the mentioned month will be displayed.

Download in pdf format
It's not over yet! There is one more thing you might want to know.
It also gives you a feature to download the monthly customer statement in pdf form. This will make it very easy for you to share monthly customer statements with your customers.
To download the report, tap on Print option and select Monthly Customer Statement.

Print >> Monthly Customer Statement
